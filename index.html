Aditya Singla - Profile

Aditya Singla is an accomplished Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), New Delhi, with a strong academic foundation, including an M.Tech and B.Tech in Computer Science from JNU. Aditya has consistently excelled academically, ranking second in the School of Engineering at JNU and achieving notable success in competitive challenges like the Amazon ML Challenge.

Aditya's research interests span Game Theory, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Optimization Techniques, among others. His M.Tech dissertation focused on using multivariate techniques to predict agricultural features, integrating remote sensing and deep learning to enhance crop yield estimations. He has also contributed to multiple research projects, including portfolio optimization using reinforcement learning and predicting stock market instabilities.

Aditya has gained valuable industry experience through internships at Tata Consultancy Services, EY, and Deutsche Telekom Digital Labs, where he worked on projects involving network traffic modeling, KPI development for government analytics, and order management systems. Additionally, he served as an assistant to a Member of Parliament, contributing to policy analysis and parliamentary work.

He holds several certifications, including in Neural Networks, Data Science, and Climate Change Mitigation from prestigious institutions like the IMF and DeepLearning.ai. Aditya is proficient in English, Hindi, and has elementary proficiency in Chinese.

Aditya has also published articles in national dailies such as The Pioneer and Millennium Post, discussing topics like eco-restoration, agriculture sector reform, and the impact of climate change. He is actively involved in research and academic discussions, having participated in the FAO-NRAA project review committee.
